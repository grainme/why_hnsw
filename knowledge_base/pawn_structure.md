Pawn structure determines the character of a position. Isolated pawns have no friendly pawns on adjacent files — they are weak because they cannot be defended by other pawns. Doubled pawns are two pawns of the same color on the same file — they limit mobility. A pawn chain is a diagonal line of pawns defending each other — attack the base of the chain. Backward pawns cannot advance safely because the adjacent pawns have already advanced past them.
