In the middlegame, piece activity is more important than material. Control open files with rooks. Place knights on outpost squares where they cannot be chased by enemy pawns. Bishops are strongest in open positions with pawns on both sides of the board. Two bishops together are called the bishop pair and are a long-term advantage. Avoid moving the same piece twice in the opening unless there is a concrete reason. Create weaknesses in the opponent's position before attacking.
