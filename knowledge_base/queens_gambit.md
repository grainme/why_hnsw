The Queen's Gambit begins with 1.d4 d5 2.c4. White offers a pawn to gain central control. It is not a true gambit because Black cannot hold the pawn safely. The Queen's Gambit Declined with 2...e6 is solid and defensive. The Queen's Gambit Accepted with 2...dxc4 gives up the center temporarily to develop freely. The Slav Defense with 2...c6 supports d5 while keeping the light-squared bishop active.
